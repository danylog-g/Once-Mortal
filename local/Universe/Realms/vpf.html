Origin


The Rebellion
   Long before the VPF was a name that made the Imperial Navy wary, the Astral Sea already bred outlaws and free spirits. The Empire of Shaire's dominance of the Endless Sea was never absolute. Abberants, monstrosities, abominations, unmapped isles, and the infinite expanse that was the Sea defied control. The first pirates, were not ideologues but survivors who refused to be ruled over. Refugees from the ancient Mind Flayer wars, refugees from Kyofu, survivors of Aerialis, romantics from the Feywild, glory-seekers from Jotunheim.
   The true birth of the VPF came with the binding of Garna. A Realm which entered the Astral Sea's network and was ablaze with its own rebellion against tyranny. Garna's rebellion understood asymmetric warfare in ways that the Gith did not. They brought the ideology, gunpowder, and hatred of expansionist imperialism. Using a loveable ideology, Jolly Roger, lead the unification and founded the VPF.


Unification
   After the VPF was established, the Empire of Shaire lost its position as the absolute force on the Sea. The Emperor withdrew from the public eye, and the Empire itself became slower. Pirate captains and admirals began appearing on the outskirts of the Empire's territory. The result was Tortuga. A location which is considered the capital of the VPF. A holysite for rebellion across the Endless Sea.
   With a vast network of Pirates and wealth, there was no need to trade with the Empire. No need to ask the Empire for aid, the VPF would assist those who supported it. No need to deal with bureacracy when claiming territory, if there are no colours, fly your own. A Brotherhood of the strongest Pirates would make and lead any anti-Imperial movement. The framework created by Jolly Roger united so many warring flags, into a loose nation of likeminded individuals.


Ideology
> Freedom from oppression and tyranny: Those who reject the Empire's machine like systems of forging soldiers and bureaucrats.
> Profits and Plunder: For every revolutionary with a cause, there are three raiders who want to get rich. The fat convoys, the crowns, the weapons, all the riches that the Empire amasses.
> Anti-Imperalism: The Empire of Shaire is an abomination, that engineers its citizens' evolution, manufactures time to age children, and treats all Realms like colonies.
> Safe Havens: The VPF shelters and protects mortals close to Godhood from the Divine Realm and the Empire; it is only a bonus if these mortals harbour a similar hatred for the Empire and Imperialism.


Fleet Autonomy
   The VPF's main method of control is the immense decentralised fleet of ships that many key figures of the VPF posess. Operating using a structure similar to the Imperial Navy, Admirals -> Commodores -> Captains and Commanders. Each fleet of three or more vessels would be considered its own faction. A fleet must consist of atleast one flagship, one warship, and one supply ship. And, of course, every Pirate who leads a faction must have a cove.
   Commodores may enforce their own codified laws, as long as the core tenets of the VPF are not destroyed. They may also recruit new members freely, and wage their own war campaigns, and raids. Admirals who oversee three or more fleets may establish their own administrative regions. Admirals with a strong following are granted a seat on the Brotherhood, allowing them to voice their opinions on long-term war with the Empire.
   In very rare cases, an Admiral may be granted a higher title of "Grand-Admiral". These titles are often reserved for Faction leaders who have amassed great swaths of territory; Or for Faction leaders who have demonstrated exceptional leadership and strategic prowess while in commands of five or more commodores.
The Brotherhood
   Every two years, or during an emergency, all members of the Brotherhood gather in Tortuga. They gather to arbitrate disputes amongst Faction leaders, decide on joint campaigns, theorize on the next steps of the Empire. Jolly Roger himself always attends these gatherings. He is the leader of the VPF and Brotherhood. All the VPF-affiliated faction leaders pledge to assist the Brotherhood and Jolly Roger in times of crisis.
Notable Members
   Jolly Roger was once a loyal servant of the Empire. Even being seated on their council. After the Empire discoverd he was secretly supporting certain Rebellious factions, they executed him. The Emperor gave Roger a hero's funeral, setting his corpse adrift on the sea. The course his vessel took, lead directly into an Astral Storm. The potent magic of the storm awakened Roger as a Lich. With his immortality and nothing to lose, Roger dedicated his existence to rebellion against the Empire.
   Henderson Always, an Astral Elf who takes on many risks when fighting sea-monsters and the Empire. He loves the feeling of adrenaline coursing thought his veins and his heart pumping quickly. Considered by many within the VPF and across the lesser Realms as the "Ideal Pirate". He was granted a seat on the council after stealing 115 million crowns from a Wild God.
   Samuel Bellamy, a scholarly High-Elf from the Feywild who constructed an incredibly large and wealthy fleet. He was granted a seat on the council for his contributions to the VPF.
   Johnathan Hawk, a Githyanki who claimed an arhcipelago of floating islands as his own. His armada of ships use advanced magic tools to sail across the skies. He is an Admiral with a seat on the Brotherhood.
   Davey Jones, an undead Mind Flayer from the ancient wars. He has a deep-seeded hatred for the Empire. While holding close to no territory, he leads a large fleet and frequently engages in battles against the Empire. He is an Admiral with a seat on the Brotherhood.
   Isabella Black, a Githzerai who uses naval spells and necromancy to lord over a large fleet. She is an Admiral in name only. Posessing no territory, she sails the edges of the Sea and Empire looking for artifacts. She has a seat on the Brotherhood.
   North Norman, an easygoing pirate admiral focused on studying the Astral Sea itself. He is known for his knowledge and for exploring the north ends of the Sea. His territory and fleet are fractured, but massive and self-sustaining. He has a seat on the Brotherhood.
   Bartholemew, a Sun High-Elf known for the sheer size of his fleet. He and his fleet reside in Tortuga, and rarely settle in territories unexplored. He was granted the Grand Admiral title for his protection of Tortuga, assistance provided to other Faction leaders, and his loyalty to the VPF. He has a seat on the Brotherhood.
